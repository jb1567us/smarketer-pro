Key Turning Points Today

Morning (6–11am): Best window for planning, outlining, or negotiating terms. Mental clarity is higher before emotional noise builds.

Midday (11am–3pm): Action window. Send the emails, make the calls, hit “publish,” schedule the meeting, make the ask.

Evening (after 7pm): Integration time. Reflect on what you did differently today and what that says about who you’re becoming.

If a decision has been looping in your mind for weeks, today wants a concrete move, even if it’s just the first visible step.

Emotional Weather

You may feel an underlying charge: a mix of excitement, impatience, and a little anxiety about getting it “right.” This is normal when you’re stepping into more power.

Expect small spikes of irritation if others move slowly or don’t match your pace. Underneath that is a deeper desire to stop wasting time and live more honestly.

Best Emotional Moves
  • Channel impatience into focused action, not conflict.
  • Use irritation as a signal: “Where am I ready to stop tolerating this?”
  • Pause for 3 deep breaths before sending any emotionally charged message.
Spiritual & Synchronicities

Today carries “yes/no” polarity. You might see clear signs—doors that open easily and doors that feel strangely jammed.

Instead of forcing jammed doors, treat resistance as information. The universe is more responsive than usual when you say: “Show me the most aligned path.”

Watch For
  • Repeating numbers or phrases around decisions you’re weighing.
  • Unexpected invitations or conversations that point to a bolder route.
  • Physical sensations (tight chest, open breath) when you imagine each option.
Money & Work Focus

This is a day to move one concrete piece of the financial puzzle, not just think about it. It favors:

  • Proposing new terms or rates.
  • Launching or announcing something you’ve already prepared.
  • Following up on a warm opportunity that’s been sitting in your inbox.
Cautions
  • Avoid “all or nothing” decisions driven by anger or pride.
  • Double-check details in contracts, invoices, or agreements before finalizing.
Ritual & Practice for March 15

Morning Power Alignment (10–15 min):

  • Stand or sit tall, feet on the floor, spine straight.
  • Place a hand on your chest and one on your solar plexus (upper stomach).
  • Say out loud three times: “Today I choose the braver aligned action, not the familiar safe one.”

Decision Altar:

  • Write your biggest current decision on a small piece of paper.
  • Place it under a candle or next to a stone/object that represents courage.
  • Light the candle and sit for 3 minutes imagining the version of you who has already made the aligned choice.
Do / Avoid Checklist
Do Today
  • Send one message or make one call you’ve been postponing.
  • Make at least one small “brave choice” (pricing, boundaries, visibility).
  • Write down any big ideas that come—capture first, refine later.
Avoid Today

  • Deciding from fear of missing out instead of grounded intuition.
  • Overloading your schedule with more commitments than you can honor.
