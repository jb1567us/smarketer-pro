Here’s a practical “landscape map” of **affiliate marketing + affiliate program management software** (plus my go-to shortlists), so you can pick the right stack without getting lost.

### 1) First: which side are you on?
Most people need *one* of these (sometimes both):

- **A) You’re a brand** (you want to *run your own affiliate program* and pay partners).
- **B) You’re a publisher/marketer** (you want to *promote offers* and track performance).

### 2) If you’re a BRAND running your own affiliate program
You’re choosing between:

#### Option 1 — Partnership/Affiliate “enterprise” platforms (powerful, $$$, lots of workflow)
Best when you need: lots of partners, complex attribution, contracts, fraud protection, multi-channel partnerships (affiliates + creators + referrals), or you’re scaling hard.

- **impact.com** — positioned as a unified partnership platform (affiliates, creators, referrals, etc.). citeturn1search3  
- **PartnerStack** — strong in B2B/SaaS partner ecosystems and networks (often mid-market+). citeturn1search24  
- **Everflow** — widely used for partner/affiliate tracking + automation (often performance teams). (See review comparisons like G2 for feature scoring.) citeturn1search20  
- **TUNE** — typically cited for granular control over tracking/payout logic and program integrity. citeturn0search3  

**When to pick this tier:** you have meaningful revenue already, expect lots of partners, and need workflow + reporting + controls.

#### Option 2 — SMB/mid-market affiliate program tools (faster setup, less heavy)
Best when you need: solid tracking, affiliate portal, payouts, coupon codes, basic fraud controls, quick launch.

- **Tapfiliate** — “launch in minutes” style affiliate platform. citeturn0search13  
- **Post Affiliate Pro** — classic affiliate program suite (tracking, payouts, performance, etc.). citeturn0search6  

**When to pick this tier:** you want to run your own program without enterprise overhead.

#### Option 3 — SaaS/subscription-first affiliate tools (simple + Stripe-friendly)
If you’re a **SaaS/subscription** business, you usually want recurring commissions + subscription attribution.

- **Rewardful** — explicitly built for SaaS affiliate + referral tracking and quick launch. citeturn1search5turn1search2  
- **FirstPromoter** — commonly compared head-to-head with Rewardful for SaaS affiliate tracking. citeturn1search8turn1search30  

**When to pick this tier:** you live in Stripe/subscriptions and want “easy mode.”
